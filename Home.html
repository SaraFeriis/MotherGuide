Pregnancy and Baby

The purpose of this web site is addressing all of new mothers’ concerns. Many pregnant women or new mothers do not have enough knowledge or experience about pregnancy and babies because some mothers do not have any relatives or friends to help them to become a good prepared mother. Therefore, we came up with the idea of creating a web site to address these problems and give some advices to new mothers about pregnancy and taking care of their babies.
